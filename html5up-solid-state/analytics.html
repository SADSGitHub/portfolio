Data Analytics in Chemical Processing Industry

In the chemical processing industry, data analytics plays a crucial role in optimizing operations, improving product quality, and reducing costs. By leveraging data from various sources such as sensors, production records, and laboratory tests, we can gain insights into processes and make data-driven decisions that can lead to significant improvements in efficiency, safety, and profitability.

Tableau Data visualization

The dashboard below was created using the Tableau data visulation studio. The data was soruced from Baton Rouge, Louisiana's publicly accessible database. The initial veiw of the map and trend charts presents the data for the entire Baton Rouge area. The trend charts are linked to the waypoints on the map and will updated when individual waypoints are selected. After a single waypoint has been selected, any of the other waypoints can be selected individually and the trends will update accordingly. To return to the initial overall view simply double click the mouse in any non-waypoint area of the map.

Data visualtions such as these are useful for strategic decision-making. In this example, decisions can be made to adjust resource allocation by season and location, or perhaps to investigate unusual spikes in occurances.
